🇺🇸 Path to Nutley, NJ

Your roadmap to an intra-company transfer at Reckitt.

```

Phase 1: Build the case (Now)

●Tell your manager you want international growth
●Volunteer for US-facing procurement projects
●Document AI Foundry impact with real metrics (savings, time, adoption)
●Build relationships with US procurement leads (virtual coffees)
●Research L-1B visa requirements (specialized knowledge)

Phase 2: Prove readiness (3–6 months)

●Lead a cross-regional project with US team
●Get promoted beyond trainee level
●Build a portfolio of your AI-driven procurement wins
●Ask HR about international mobility programs
●Get a sponsor/mentor on the US side

Phase 3: Make the ask (6–12 months)

●Formal transfer request with business justification
●Propose specific role/projects for Nutley
●Show how your AI expertise fills a US team gap
●Negotiate visa sponsorship as part of transfer
●Align timing with your trainee program completion

Skills that make you transferable

AI/Copilot Studio Should-cost modeling Supplier analytics Python/Low-code Cross-cultural fluency Procurement negotiation Stakeholder management Data-driven decisions
```
